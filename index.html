Guide

The seed philosophy

Seeds are sovereignty

A crypto seed phrase gives you self-custodial control over your financial assets — no bank, no government, no intermediary. Heirloom seeds give you food sovereignty — the ability to grow, save, and share seeds without dependence on commercial seed companies. Both represent independence.

Redundancy is resilience

One backup isn't enough. Store your crypto seed phrase in multiple secure locations using durable materials. Keep your seed collection in multiple storage environments with proper temperature and humidity control. Single points of failure are how people lose everything.

Knowledge is protection

Understanding how seed phrases work (BIP-39, derivation paths) protects you from social engineering attacks. Understanding germination rates, cross-pollination, and seed viability protects your garden's genetic future. In both domains, education is your best defense.

Essential resources

BIP-39 Wordlist

The official 2,048-word list used by most cryptocurrency wallets to generate seed phrases. Understanding this standard helps you verify your wallet's integrity. View BIP-39 wordlist

Seed Savers Exchange

A nonprofit dedicated to preserving heirloom and open-pollinated seed varieties. Their collection includes thousands of rare varieties you can't find in commercial catalogs. Visit Seed Savers Exchange

Svalbard Global Seed Vault

The world's backup seed bank, storing duplicates of seed collections from around the globe in a frozen mountain vault in Norway. A fascinating model for thinking about long-term preservation. Learn about Svalbard
